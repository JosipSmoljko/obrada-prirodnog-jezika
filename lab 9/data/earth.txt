Earth is the third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to support life. Earth rotates on its axis, creating day and night, and orbits the Sun once every 365 days. Earth is part of the Solar System, which also includes Mercury, Venus, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, and Neptune. Scientists study Earth to understand climate, weather patterns, natural resources, and the impact of human activities on the planet. Observing Earth from space.
